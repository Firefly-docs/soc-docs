# RK3576

## Key features

- Octa-core 64-bit high-performance ARM processor, with rich high-speed interfaces such as PCIe/USB3.0/SATA/GMAC and low-speed interfaces such as CAN FD/DSMC/UART/SPI/I2C/I3C. One platform can be quickly deployed with multiple applications.
- 6 TOPS self-developed high-efficiency AI NPU to support various AI applications.
- Rich display interface, high-performance GPU, supporting 3 displays with different sources.
- Strong image perception, video coding and decoding, and audio processing capabilities, support interactive visual and voice recognition. Support standard Android and Linux SDKs, support Chinese OS.
