# RV1126B

## Key features

- 4K high-performance low-power AI image processor
- Quad ARM Cortex-A53
- Configure 3 TOPs NPU to support large models up to 2B
- Support 12M@30fps ISP, 8M@30fps AI-ISP
- Support AI Remosaic、PDAF
- Multi camera AI dynamic stitching (2 * 6M binocular stitching/4 * 2M panoramic stitching)
- Supports simultaneous input from 5 cameras
- Support 4K@45fps H. 264/H.265 encoding and 4K@30 decoding
